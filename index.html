About Us

Mandira Path is a youth-led heritage initiative dedicated to bringing India's temple architecture, culture, and hidden sacred stories into the digital world. We aim to preserve the beauty of ancient mandirs, celebrate regional diversity, and inspire the younger generation to reconnect with our spiritual roots.

From Dravidian stone marvels of the South to the majestic Nagara temples of the North and the innovative modern temples of today's era—our mission is to document, educate, and celebrate India's sacred heritage with authenticity and creativity.
